Know more About Us !

Pet Pal is a website that provides the opportunity for users to adopt or rehome their pets, and/or to provide care for stray animals through nearby animal shelters. We have numerous dogs and cats on our website that the user can choose to adopt from. Our rehoming service helps owners provide a new home for their pets. The animal assistance section aids the user in sending details of any stray animals found to the local shelters. Hence they are able to send provisions (and/or first aid) for the said animals.

Acts and Licenses

The basic cruelty law of India is contained in the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act 1960. The objective of the Act is to prevent the infliction of unnecessary pain or suffering on animals and to amend the laws relating to the prevention of cruelty to animals.
