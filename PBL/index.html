About PetPal

PetPal is a small effort, by and for animal-lovers, to connect the animals to active animal shelters, fosters and maybe their new forever homes. We act as a bridge between shelters and individuals to enable a smoother flow of communication. We also provide a way to link any strays found to the closest shelters for a safe and caring temporary abode.
